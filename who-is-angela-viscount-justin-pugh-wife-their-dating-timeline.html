Angela Viscount, the wife of Justin Pugh, a professional NFL football player, hails from Newark, Delaware.

She’s built her own identity separate from her husband’s high-profile career.

Angela is a versatile individual with a background in fitness, acrobatics, fashion, and merchandising.

Angela Viscount Early Life and Education

Her athletic journey began at the young age of three when she started practicing gymnastics.

As she grew older, she transitioned into competitive cheerleading, participating in an all-star cheer squad from ages seven to eleven.

Throughout middle school, she continued to cheer, further nurturing her passion for sports. For high school, Angela attended Padua Academy, an all-girls Catholic high school in Wilmington, Delaware.

Interestingly, her high school didn’t have a cheerleading program, so she returned to the world of all-star competitive cheering.

Angela’s academic and athletic pursuits eventually led her to Fairmont State University, where she studied fashion merchandising and became a member of the Falcon’s acrobatics and tumbling team.

During her college years, she interned at Bloomingdale’s, gaining valuable experience in the fashion industry.

Afterward, in 2014, she worked as a fashion stylist, showcasing her dedication to the fashion world.

Angela’s professional journey continued with roles as a sales assistant and junior account executive at 2(X)IST.

Later, she worked as a global merchandising assistant at Calvin Klein, highlighting her impressive career growth in the fashion industry.
