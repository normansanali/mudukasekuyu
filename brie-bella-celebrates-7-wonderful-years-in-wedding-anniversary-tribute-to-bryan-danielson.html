Brie Bella celebrates '7 wonderful years' in wedding anniversary tribute to Bryan Danielson

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  479 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

They married on April 11, 2014, and now have two children together.

And on Sunday, Brie Bella celebrated her seventh wedding anniversary with a sweet tribute to husband Bryan Danielson on Instagram.  

‘7 wonderful years with my SweetFace,’ she wrote as she shared a montage of photos from their wedding dance to today.

‘my SweetFace’: Brie Bella celebrated her seventh wedding anniversary with a sweet tribute to husband Bryan Danielson on Instagram on Sunday

‘Nothing is better than experiencing life with you. Love you @bryanldanielson Happy Anniversary,’ Brie, 37, wrote.

The two WWE stars got engaged in 2013.

On August 1 last year, Brie gave birth to her second child with Danielson (a.k.a. Daniel Bryan), 39 – a son Buddy.

They are also parents to three-year-old daughter Birdie.

Mom and dad: Brie, 37, and Bryan, 39, are parents to three-year-old daughter Birdie

Their baby: The couple welcomed their son Buddy on August 1 last year

Last month, the Total Bellas reality star had proudly shown off what she called her ‘treasure marks’ on her Instagram.

She revealed the reality of having carried and then delivered via C-section two babies in a candid mirror selfie displaying her bare stomach.

Showing off her belly, Brie wrote: ‘The more baby weight I lose the more my treasure marks from Buddy are showing up. Buddy pretty much grew through my belly button lol I guess the old wives tales were right how boys grow! Low!!’ 

Brie continued: ‘After my second C section I’ve been really kicking my butt to get my abs back! And sooo is the saggy stomach which is normal after babies. 

‘Sometimes it’s hard for me to look in the mirror and see my body. I always tell myself to be kind and grateful but I’m human so picking myself apart happens.’ 

The mom of two added: ‘You might see me at the pool in my one piece but I’m showing all of you that my treasure marks are something special. They remind me of a special connection I got to share with my sweet babies for 9 months.’
