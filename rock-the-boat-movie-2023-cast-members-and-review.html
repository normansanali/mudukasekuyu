Rock The Boat Plot Synopsis

The movie centers around Millie. She lost her parents ten years ago in an accident. Eventually, she started picking herself up and is now the most popular girl in the school.

However, all is not well in Millie’s world. She has a close group of friends who begin to fall into tragedy one after the other.

It all starts with Adam, who overdoses. Lewis follows as he slips, resulting in a broken neck. Bree falls off the balcony as her heels break while Terri meets with an accident.

Even though all of these seem like accidents, Millie suspects that there is more than meets the eye. She starts receiving letters that indicates that maybe her friends did not pass away from accidents after all.

Cynthia Bailey comes around as Aunt Carole to remind her of childhood flashbacks as things begin to unravel. However, you’ll have to watch the movie to figure all of this out.

Prepare yourself for a cliffhanger ending, which makes you wonder, will there be a part 2?
