Who is Robin Roberts?

Meet Robin Roberts, a highly accomplished American television broadcaster renowned for co-anchoring the beloved show “Good Morning America” on ABC.

Born on November 23, 1960, she initially flourished in sports broadcasting before embracing her role in the morning show.

Prior to joining “GMA,” Roberts served as a sports anchor for esteemed local TV and radio stations, and her expertise as a sportscaster on ESPN spanned an impressive 15 years.

Beyond her broadcasting triumphs, Robin’s notable contributions to basketball earned her a well-deserved spot in the prestigious Women’s Basketball Hall of Fame in 2012.

Her warm and charismatic presence has endeared her to audiences, solidifying her position as a beloved figure in the world of television.
