Kim Kardashian Steps Out in Japan in a Beige Look and Rare Birkin

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kim Kardashian was spotted out and about in Japan with her kids on Tuesday, looking stylish in a monochrome ensemble to attend a pro soccer match.

The Skims founder went to see Saudi Arabia’s Al Nassr club play France’s Paris Saint-Germain in Osaka, and documented the experience in her Instagram Stories, even sharing a photo of her son Saint West with soccer star Cristiano Ronaldo.

For the occasion, Kardashian dressed in her best stadium look, composed of a skintight beige scoop-neck bodysuit, grayish beige motocross-style Balenciaga pants with ribbed detailing at the knees, pointy cobalt-blue heels, and a rare Hermès Birkin bag. The handbag seems to be an ivory-and-brown croc-embossed Himalaya Birkin with white gold hardware, made by dyeing the skin from a Nile crocodile, and retails for anywhere from $150,000 to $600,000. (The diamond-encrusted version of the It bag was once named the most expensive and coveted Birkin in the world.) Himalaya refers not to the origins of the bag, but to its coloring.

Splash News

Kardashian completed the look with her usual bronzy glam and a slicked-back ponytail.

The reality star has been exploring Japan with her kids since attending the soccer match, and has shared photos and videos of their trip all over her Instagram Story. Today, she posted snaps from a visit to an aquarium, as well as shots of colorful snacks and views.

She also spent some time in Japan with her four kids last April and even debuted a fun all-pink look with her girls while touring Tokyo.
