Kanye West Announces Nas' Album Release Date

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  113 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kanye West has announced more Good news on Twitter. The man of the hour has let his 13.9 million followers know that Nas will be reemerging on the scene with a new album dropping on June 15th, produced by yours truly. Nas' upcoming tape is a follow-up to his 2012 LP Life is Good. 

The news follows his previous album announcements - Pusha T’s new album out May 25th, a new solo 7-track album out June 1st, a collaborative album with Kid Cudi out June 8th, and Teyana Taylor’s anticipated album out June 22nd. "I'm hand producing all the albums I tweeted about. Been chopping samples from the sunken place," he said in another tweet.
