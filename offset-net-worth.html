American rapper, songwriter, and singer Offset’s net worth is a staggering $26 million.

Originally Kiari Kendrell Cephus, Offset was born in Lawrenceville, Georgia, on December 14, 1991. Abandoned by his father at a young age, Offset was raised by his aunt, along with his cousins.

He is best known as a member of the hip-hop group Migos, with rappers Quavo and Takeoff. 

Offset is married to Grammy award-winning rapper Cardi B. They have two children together.

He grew up along with other members of Migos in Gwinnett County, listening to artists like Lil Wayne, Outkast, Gucci Mane, Master P, and Hot Boys.

In 2017, Offset released ‘Ric Flair Drip,’ his first platinum solo single. The song was from the collab album with 21 Savage and Metro Boomin, ‘Without Warning.’

Offset | Career

After founding the group with Takeoff and Quavo in 2008, the trio released their first mixtape, ‘Juug Season,’ in 2011.

In 2013, the group started gaining popularity with their single ‘Versace,’ followed by their 2015- debut studio album ‘Yung Rich Nation.’ The album reached no. 17 on the Billboard 200 chart.

The trio’s single ‘Bad and Boujee’ featuring Lil Uzi Vert went viral in 2017, gaining global recognition.

After that, they released their second studio album, ‘Culture,’ which debuted at no. 1 on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified platinum by the RIAA, followed by ‘Culture II’ in 2018.

In 2017, Offset released a collaborative album with rapper 21 Savage and producer Metro Boomin, ‘Without Warning.’ The album peaked at no. 4 on the US Billboard 200 chart.

He has featured on several tracks like Metro Boomin’s single ‘No Complain’ with Drake, Macklemore’s song ‘Willy Wonka,’ Tyga’s song ‘Taste,’ and so on. 

In 2019, Offset released his first solo album, ‘Father of 4’.

The album features artists like J. Cole, Gucci Mane, Big Rube, CeeLo Green, Gunna, and Cardi B. It was a critically well-acclaimed album and reached no. 4 on the US Billboard 200 chart.

In 2021, the Migos released their fourth studio album ‘Culture III’ which features many artists like Drake, Future, Cardi B, Polo G, Justin Bieber, Juice Wrld, and others.
