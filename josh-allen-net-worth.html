Josh Allen | Career

Josh Allen started his college football career in 2015 with the Wyoming Cowboys. After sustaining a season-ending injury in his first college game, he won the starting spot the following season.

After just being drafted in the first round by the Buffalo Bills in 2018, he took the first step in his professional career at the early age of 22.

Josh started as a quarterback alongside AJ McCarron and Nathan Peterman. The young player was named captain for the Buffalo Bills’ season opener against the New York Jets.
