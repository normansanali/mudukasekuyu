WOO HOOOOOOO!!!! The original Bravo drama mamas are back! Or at least they will be on April 14 when The Real Housewives of Orange County Season 9 premieres with lots of new faces. Show stalwarts Tamra Judge and Vicki Gunvalson are back, as is Heather Dubrow. Meanwhile, Alexis Bellino, Lydia McLaughlin, Gretchen Rossi and honorary Housewife Slade Smiley are all gone and will be replaced by Shannon Beador, Lizzie Rovsek and “Friend of the Housewives” Danielle Gregorio.

Bravo has just released the first preview trailer for the new season and it looks like more of what RHOC fans have come to expect: road trips, cattiness, newbie hazing, drankin’ and healthy doses of both T as well as A:
