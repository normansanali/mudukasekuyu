The Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3 multiplayer is live, allowing franchise fans to check out what’s new. In classic Call of Duty fashion, players can level up their operators and unlock new weapons. If you are here, you’re probably wondering what the max level is for Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3. This guide will cover everything you need to know.

Max Level for Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3 Beta

According to the official Call of Duty website, players can obtain a maximum level of 55 and then have a choice of entering into Prestige Rank 1. Once players enter Prestige Rank 1 in Modern Warfare 3, they can progress another set amount levels until they reach Prestige Rank 2. The maximum Prestige Rank is 5 for Season 1 of Call of Duty MW3. Check out all the levels for each Prestige Rank below.

  • Prestige 1: Rank 56
  • Prestige 2: Rank 100
  • Prestige 3: Rank 150
  • Prestige 4: Rank 200
  • Prestige 5: Rank 250
