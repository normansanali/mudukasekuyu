LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: Twitch celebrity Imane 'Pokimane' Anys took to Instagram to share a video showcasing her haircare routine, aimed at maintaining and enhancing her beautiful curls. She delighted her fans by sharing her naturally curly hair.

Over time, Pokimane has openly discussed the criticism she faced due to her hair, but she persevered and established her unique style within the content creator community, proudly embracing her curly locks. Her followers showered her with over two hundred thousand likes, demonstrating their admiration and support.

'My curly hair routine'
