Income From His Career As Pastor

It has been over two decades since Andy Stanley is serving as a Pastor. He started being involved in Church activities professionally as a youth minister at his father’s church. Later, when First Baptist Church opened its first satellite church on 1992’s Easter Sunday, his dad, Charles Stanley, appointed him as its pastor.

After working at First Baptist Church for three years, Andy Stanley, with his wife, Sandra Stanley, founded North Point Ministry in 1995. Ever since then, he is serving as NPM’s Senior Pastor. Stanley’s ministry currently consists of a total of seven churches in the Atlanta area and a network of over a hundred churches worldwide. NPM collectively provides service to nearly 185K people every week.

Furthermore, as mentioned in PayScale, a Senior Pastor gets an average salary of $61 667 yearly with a bonus of up to 14K. Moreover, they also get some share from the Church’s profit where they serve as a Pastor. So, Andy Stanley also might be getting a similar amount for his service in NPM as its Senior Pastor.
