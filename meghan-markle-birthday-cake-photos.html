Meghan Markle Celebrates Birthday with Cake from Luminary Bakery

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, celebrated her 38th birthday on August 4, 2019.
  • Prince Harry wished his "amazing wife" a happy birthday with an adorable Instagram post.
  • Royal correspondent Omid Scobie has now given fans a glimpse of Meghan's incredible birthday carrot cake.

Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, turned 38 last week, and she celebrated in style.

Related Story

Having received birthday wishes from members of the royal family on social media, Meghan celebrated her special day at home with a "family day," a source told BAZAAR.com. Fans hoping to see a photo of Duchess Meghan on her birthday were sadly disappointed, but her gorgeous birthday cake has finally been revealed.

Royal correspondent Omid Scobie shared a photo of Meghan's cake on Twitter and wrote, "Thanks so much to Alice Williams and the awesome team at @LuminaryBakery in London for letting us join them last weekend as they created Duchess Meghan’s beautiful carrot birthday cake." Unsurprisingly, the cake looked absolutely scrumptious.

Meghan included Luminary Bakery in British Vogue's September 2019 issue, which she guest edited. The royal highlighted the organization as one of her "Forces for Change," and it's easy to see why.

Reporting on Luminary Bakery, Good Morning America wrote, "She was impressed at how the bakery, which describes itself as a social enterprise, teaches women who are homeless, have been in the prison system, are victims of domestic violence, or in the sex trade, how to bake—and find employment."

And judging by how beautiful Meghan's birthday cake was, the bakery is sure to be inundated with orders.
