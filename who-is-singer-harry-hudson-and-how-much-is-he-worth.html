Who Is Harry Hudson?

Harry Hudson first came about as a close family friend to none other than the Kar-Jenner clan. The star is most known for being besties with Kylie Jenner, whom he met after moving to Los Angeles from Englewood, New Jersey.

His move across the country was fueled by his passion for music, and it certainly paid off! Harry has always taken a liking to singing and songwriting, so it was only a matter of time before he ventured off to the city of dreams.

During his time in LA, Harry began to make a name for himself on social media, which ultimately landed him in Kylie Jenner's close circle. The duo has been stuck to the hip ever since, and Harry has gone on to appear in a handful of Keeping Up With The Kardashians episodes.

Despite appearing in the hit E! series, Harry revealed that he's yet to watch a single episode of the show.

In 2014, the singer fell ill with Hodgkin Lymphoma and underwent a series of treatments before getting a clean bill of health. Hudson revealed that during his fight, Kylie and older sis, Kendall visited quite often!

"Those two are a blessing, [They] are like my sisters. They dropped so many things just to be there and be a positive thing in my life," Harry said during an interview with VICE.

Harry went on to use his journey as the inspiration behind some of his first releases, including "Learn 2 Love", and "Treatment A-Side". After releasing a series of EP's, the singer officially signed with Roc Nation in 2018 and released his very first album.

Luckily for Harry, his success in the music industry has allowed him to amass a net worth of an estimated $1 million, which is only set to go up!

In 2020, Harry released his sophomore album, Hey, I'm Here For You, which has garnered a collective 35 million streams from between the 15 tracks.

In addition to his most recent release, Harry is also readying the launch of his very own therapy foundation! The star has previously worked with Teen Cancer America where he oversaw a number of projects that he's launched for the foundation.

While Harry has had to overcome a handful of hurdles over the course of his young adult life, the star has his eyes set for major things, which includes his upcoming North American tour with fellow musician, Alec Benajmin.
