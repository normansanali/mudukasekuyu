Biography of Cassandra Sánchez Navarro

Beginnings in the life of Cassandra Sánchez Navarro

The actress Cassandra Sánchez Navarro is the daughter of the also interpreter of great role Mónica Sánchez-Navarro, niece of the actor Rafael Sánchez-Navarro and granddaughter of the renowned actor of the Golden Age of Mexican cinema Manolo Fábregas. From a very young age and seeing that all her relatives and people close to her were dedicated to acting, she knew that she should also continue in that line and not precisely because it was something imposed but because acting was also something that fascinated Cassandra Sánchez Navarro.

When the time came to decide what she would dedicate herself to, Cassandra Sánchez Navarro did not hesitate and decided to study acting at the Center for Artistic Education of Televisa (CEA) where she left being one of the best in her generation in 2011 and just out of her great professional preparation she entered one of the most important projects of those years in Mexican television, A lucky family.

Trajectory and history of Cassandra Sánchez Navarro

Una familia con suerte is a Mexican telenovela produced by Juan Osorio for Televisa. Version of the Argentine telenovela Los Roldán. In this story starring Arath de la Torre, Mayrín Villanueva and Luz Elena González, Cassandra Sánchez Navarro had her first opportunity on television playing the role of Salomé, which although it did not have much relevance within the main plot, was a great experience that would give her her first approach to the famous melodramas of Televisa.

The following projects that came out for Cassandra Sánchez Navarro continued to be in the soap operas of the channel of the stars, in 2102 she appeared in “Corona de lagrimas” playing Consuelo María del Pilar “Chelito” Durán Requena, the actress for her great performance within the melodrama “Corona de lágrimas” was nominated for the TVyNovelas awards in the category of “Best female revelation” and finally on the day of the awards she took the statuette.

A year later she appeared in “Quiero amarte” playing the role of Flavia Montesinos Ugarte and in 2015 “La sombra del pasado” would arrive, a telenovela produced by MaPat López de Zatarain for Televisa; starring Michelle Renaud and Pablo Lyle, where Cassandra Sánchez Navarro appeared as Verónica Santos. While making those melodramas the actress also tried her luck in the cinema appearing in some films that did not have much transcendence such as “A broke code, Gabe the cupid Dig, A selfie”, among others.

Cassandra Sánchez Navarro in 2015 decided to take the step to the series and appeared in SENSE8 an American science fiction web series produced by Netflix and created, written and directed by the Wachowski Sisters and J. Michael Straczynski which had two seasons and she participated being the beloved Nuri. In 2016 he returned to telenovelas but now in the United States on the Univision network appearing in the soap opera YAGO and in 2018 he was in La bella y las bestias a telenovela produced by W Studios in co-production with Lemon Studios for Televisa and Univision, as well as in Las Buchonas.

At the beginning of 2019 after being postponed for a long time came the series of “Silvia Pinal, frente a ti” produced by Carla Estrada for Televisa and based on the life of the great Mexican actress Silvia Pinal. Cassandra Sánchez Navarro played the role of one of the key pieces in the life of the actress, playing Viridiana Alatriste who was the first daughter of Silvia Pinal but who died tragically at 19 years of age because of a car accident after her car fell down a ravine after returning from a party.

El Dragón: El regreso de un guerrero (or simply El Dragón) was a crime drama telenovela produced by W Studios for Televisa and Netflix in 2019 starring Sebastián Rulli and Renata Notni in which Cassandra Sánchez Navarro had one of the most memorable roles playing Francisca “Chisca” Garza Martínez. On this role that meant a great moment for her artistic career the actress commented in an interview: “It was a very strong character, to be honest I got to have many nightmares, it was traumatic since the girl is capricious, despot, aggressive, destructive and victim of addictions, she came home crying, very worn out because I really got into the character a lot. “

In October 2019, Cassandra Sánchez-Navarro was chosen to star in the montage, with which the actress paid tribute to her grandfather (Manolo) and grandmother, Fela Fábregas; being the protagonist of the musical Sugar. About his participation in this staging he commented: “The whole story of how he came out, now do it with Arath (de la Torre), who with him had my first scene on television, in a prison, that still makes it more incredible, that motivates me to work 30 times more than with anyone else, because I want to fill my shoes and have fun, It would be wrong if I just wanted to make it perfect,” the actress said. In Sugar Cassandra Sánchez-Navarro shared the stage with Arath de la Torre and Ariel Miramontes.

At the end of 2019 Cassandra Sánchez Navarro was invited by Galerías Plaza de las Estrellas to capture her footprints, due to the great work she was doing with great success as an actress. Her great merit of capturing her hands in one of the most prestigious squares of Mexico City and that houses the traces of great personalities and figures of the international show undoubtedly for her was a moment of great happiness dedicating to her mother such recognition. “To my mom that although there are times when I have fought with her, because it happens, the families fight and we are happy and you surely … there is my mom,” he said when the actress and singer, Monica Sanchez Navarro, appeared surprisingly in the audience.

In January 2020, Cindy La Regia was released throughout Mexico, a film starring Cassandra Sánchez Navarro that tells the story of an attractive 24-year-old woman from the city of Monterrey who found herself in the dilemma of accepting or not marrying her boyfriend, since all her life she has wanted it. But once the time has come, he begins to doubt whether he should really do it or not. The producers of this film project commented that they had a hard time finding the actress who would play Cindy in this film because nobody gave the width until they finally met Cassandra Sánchez Navarro and could not do better so they chose her as the protagonist of this story of drama and comedy.

Cindy La Regia had Cassandra Sánchez Navarro in the leading role and shared credits with other great actors such as Giuseppe Gamba, Regina Blandón, Diana Bovio, Roberto Quijano, Nicolasa Ortiz Monasterio, Diego Amozurrutia, Alexandra de la Mora, Enoc Leaño, Carlos Gatica, Mayra Batalla, Ceci de la Cueva, Marianna Burelli, Isela Vega, and Martha Debayle.

At the end of 2020, the second season of ¿Quién es la máscara? a television program where certain personalities of the show performed songs under some costume to hide their true personality and appearance; in the second season Omar Chaparro was the official driver while the team of investigators was made up of Yuri, Consuelo Duval, Carlos Rivera and JuanPa Zurita who were in charge of investigating who was behind the masked characters. In the first chapter the masked character “Unicorn” was presented and although after giving several clues many thought that it was several personalities finally sang it was confirmed that “Unicorn Cassandra Sánchez Navarro was in Who is the mask?”.

One of the great successes achieved in his career occurred in mid-2022 when “Corona de lágrimas 2” premiered, a dramatic web series in telenovela format premiered exclusively for Televisa produced by José Alberto Castro “El Güero Castro” and based 10 years after the events of the first season launched in 2012 and that was a success on Mexican television so they did not hesitate to do The sequel. In the series “Corona de lágrimas 2” the actress Cassandra Sánchez Navarro played Consuelo María del Pilar Durán Requena “Chelito”.
