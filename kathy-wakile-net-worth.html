Katherina Pierri was born on 4 October 1965, in Paterson, New Jersey USA, of  part Italian ancestry, and  known as Kathy Wakile, is an entrepreneur as well as a well-known reality TV personality.

So just how rich is Kathy Wakile, as of late 2017? Such fame has no doubt been a valuable asset for Kathy, as a familiar name is really beneficial in developing her own business.

Little is known of Kathy’s life prior to her debut in “The Real Housewives of New Jersey”. Kathy Wakile is the wife of businessman Richard “Richie” Wakile, who is also seen in the reality show. So to talk about her subsequent career, Kathy decided to start her own business, and now markets three product lines – she first became famous in 2012 for her Dolci Della Dea , which includes a cannoli kit, described as authentic Italian. Wakile has also written a cookbook – “Indulge: Delicious Little Desserts That Keep Life Real Sweet”, which has contributed to her worth too. The reality show also revealed Kathy Wakile‘s dream to found her own restaurant one day, and this she launched in September 2017, called Pizza Love – no prizes for guessing the cuisine! Her husband is also in on the investment, located in Bergen County. New Jersey.
