Ronda Rousey Isnt Signing With AEW, Wants To Be A Full-Time Mom

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  286 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ronda Rousey isn’t getting the ‘All Elite’ graphic any time soon.

TMZ Sports spoke with Ronda Rousey about her future in the pro wrestling business. Rousey recently wrestled for Wrestling Revolver and Ring Of Honor, the latter of which caused speculation that she could sign with AEW.

“No, no, I don’t think so,” Ronda replied. “Just trying to be home with the family, and be a full-time mom.”

Rousey was also asked about her acting career, but said “full-time mom” when asked about any more potential on-screen roles.

“Just trying to be home with the family, and be a full-time mom,” Rousey answered.

Why ROH?

Ronda Rousey teamed up with her best friend, Marina Shafir to defeat the team of Athena and Billie Starkz in her ROH debut. Following the episode, The Baddest Woman on the Planet had a few words of encouragement for Shafir.

“Marina, as your best friend I want you to have so much fun doing this. And I came here to personally make sure that you had a really good time today. Also, it wasn’t out of the way, I like live here as well so I just got to stop by. But it was a really good time. And you just keep having a good time without me here. Even though it is never going to be as much as a good time because I’m your f****** best friend. And I totally swore so I guess… Like it’s okay. You just get used to having not as good as a time as you normally would when I’m around. But still, have that much of a good time you know, because I want that for you because I love you,” Ronda Rousey said.
