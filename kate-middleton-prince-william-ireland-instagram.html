 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Prince William and Kate Middleton shared a sweet photo to commemorate the close of their three-day trip to Ireland this week.
  • The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge toured the cities of Dublin, County Meath, County Kildare, and Galway during their tour of the country.
  • The royal couple are scheduled to reunite with Prince Harry and Duchess Meghan next week for Commonwealth Day in London.

The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge are reminiscing on the end of their Ireland visit with an adorable Instagram.

The snap shows Prince William and Kate Middleton standing side by side and embracing while they pose on a scenic hilltop overlooking the Ireland coast.
