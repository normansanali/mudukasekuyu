Jungkook may have cut his hair, but the Earth continues to spin

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

BTS star, Jungkook surprised fans in a recent live stream with a brand new look. While the revelation might sound like the end of the world, we can assure you that everything is fine.

The K-pop star went live on Weverse, where he shared life updates, such as his goal of wanting to learn guitar, and gave advice to a fan who just finished their homework and has no idea what to do next. During the stream, fans noticed that Jungkook looked slightly different. It seems like the celebrity cut his precious locks, which surprised fans because it was something they didn’t expect.

Fortunately, Jungkook was aware of the fans’ shock and addressed the trim. He said that he will be “slowly cutting his hair” until he’s no longer recognizable. Not entirely sure if this was a reference to the stalking threats he’s received. Regardless, while fans were surprised by this decision, they’re not entirely sure if it really was a cut or a trim.

Perhaps his haircut announcement is to have fans prepared for how he’s going to look once he starts his South Korean military duties in 2024. After all, K-pop fans were shocked to see photos of Jin with his hair shaved once he started his mandatory enlistment last year. So it won’t be long now until fans will see photos of a fully-shaven Jungkook once he begins his duties.

For now, let’s appreciate the fact that he still tried to keep his precious locks on his head and that it’s a slow process that he plans to ease fans into.
