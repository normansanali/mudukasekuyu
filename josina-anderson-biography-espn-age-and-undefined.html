Josina Anderson Biography; ESPN, Age And Undefined

Josina Anderson is an award-winning American sports reporter for ESPN and Fox Sports.

Josina Anderson was born on 15th August 1978 in Washington D.C, Unites States. She is the daughter of Yasmin Anderson and Lloyd Anderson.

Josina Anderson started her education at the Montgomery Blair High School in Maryland and later got a degree in exercise and sports science from the University of North Carolina.

Aside from her work as a sports reporter, she is generally interested in sports. While at school, she competed in the 200 and 400-meter races and she won a gold medal in AAU Junior Olympics.

Josina Anderson ESPN

Josina Anderson has been working with ESPN, a sports network for over nine years as a Chicago-based reporter and was named the named ESPN’s first female NFL insider in 2015. There were rumors in 2020 about Josina Anderson parting ways with ESPN which later proved to be untrue.
