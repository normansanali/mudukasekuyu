These Big Brother Cast Members Have Made A Fortune Since Being On The Show

Reality TV has become a major source of entertainment in recent years, and Big Brother is one of the most popular shows in this genre. The show has given us many memorable contestants who have gone on to make a name for themselves outside the house.

While some former Big Brother players have faded into obscurity, others have managed to turn their time on the show into a lucrative career. From social media influencers to business owners, many Big Brother players have found success and fortune after the cameras stopped rolling.

From leveraging their reality TV fame to building successful businesses, these players have proven that a stint on Big Brother can be a stepping stone to bigger and better things. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the most financially successful post-Big Brother careers of former contestants.

8 How Much Money Has Rachel Reilly Made Since Winning Big Brother?

Rachel is probably one of the better-known cast mates from Big Brother. Not only did she meet her husband during her first season on the show, but she also came back for season 13 and won.

Since winning, she has gone on to become a certified reality TV star, appearing on shows like The Amazing Race, Celebrity Fear Factor, and Peacock's newest reality show, The Traitors. She has also worked as a TV host and has become a successful influencer and brand ambassador, earning a reported net worth of around $500,000.

7 How Much Money Have Jeff Schroeder and Jordan Lloyd Made Since Big Brother?

Jeff and Jordan easily became fan favorites on Big Brother 11. It was hard not to route for the adorable couple. Fans were hopeful that the showmance would outlast the Big Brother house, unlike so many others. While it took some effort, Jeff and Jordan have been together since leaving the show and are now happily married with children.

The pair returned to The Big Brother house for season 13 and also went on to compete on The Amazing Race. They have also worked as TV hosts and have amassed a combined net worth of around $5 million since being on Big Brother. They're also still super close with the production team at Big Brother, with Jeff hosting an official podcast for the show.

6 How Much Money Has Frankie Grande Made Since Big Brother?

Frankie Grande was a fan favorite during his time competing on Big Brother 16 - and not just because his sister is pop superstar Ariana Grande. The show allowed Frankie to be his own person, with the reality tv star not telling his housemates about his famous sister. While he didn't win, he made it incredibly far during his season.

He certainly made a name for himself. Since appearing on the show, he's become a successful social media personality and performer in his own right. He has worked as a Broadway actor and has released music and other content on his YouTube channel, earning a reported net worth of around $1.5 million.

5 How Much Money Has Nicole Franzel Made Since Big Brother?

Nicole Franzel has become a polarizing Big Brother player; you either love her or hate her. Funnily enough, her sweet-natured personality has led fans of the show to feel this way about her, too, with many fans questioning whether she deserved to win her season or compete as an all-star.

The hate hasn't held Nicole back, though. If anything, it's made her a stronger Big Brother competitor and overall woman. She met the love of her life in the house, as well, as her housemate Victor Arroyo. While the two never dated in the house, they connected years after and fell deeply in love. They have one child together and are often seen posting cute pictures and videos of their family on Instagram.

Along with competing on multiple seasons of Big Brother, Nicole has also appeared on The Amazing Race. She has become a successful influencer and brand ambassador, working with companies like Olay and Clorox. She also runs her own podcast and has a reported net worth of around $5 million.

4 How Much Money Has Dan Gheesling Made Since Big Brother?

Dan Gheelsing is one of the most infamous players in Big Brother history. Players frequently referenced him on the show, and fans hold him in his regard as well. He won Big Brother 10 and later returned for Big Brother 14, where he came in second to player Ian Terry, which made him the only Big Brother player to appear in the final two twice.

Since his Big Brother days, Dan has become a successful author and speaker, publishing several books on leadership and motivation. He has also worked as a college football coach. According to online sources, Dan has a reported net worth of around $5 million.

3 How Much Money Has Derrick Levasseur Made Since Big Brother?

Derrick won Big Brother 16 thanks to his detective skills and has since become a successful TV host and author thanks to them as well. Prior to joining the Big Brother cast, Derrick worked as a police officer. He's since retired, thanks to his reality tv days, and is using his experience on both the show and in the police force to create a consulting company that trains law enforcement agencies on interrogation techniques. Derrick also hosted a true-crime show on Oxygen for two seasons. Since Big Brother, Derrick has amassed a reported net worth of around $2 million.

2 How Much Money Has Josh Martinez Made Since Big Brother?

Despite his pots and pans solo routine that shook both the house and dedicated Big Brother fans to their core, Josh Martinez went on to win his season of Big Brother. He's shown a lot of growth since then, too, becoming friends with his enemies on the show. He continued after the show to become a successful reality TV personality and influencer. He has appeared on several other reality shows, including The Challenge and Ex on the Beach. According to online sources, Josh has a reported net worth between $1 and $5 million.

1 How Much Has Paul Abrahamian Made Since Big Brother?

Forever the runner-up, Paul came in second on his consecutive seasons of Big Brother. He's still friends with the numerous members of the cast who voted against his win as well, despite his dual failed attempts at taking the crown.

Since Big Brother, Paul diverted from the reality tv pathway, becoming a successful clothing designer. He launched his own line, Deadskull Apparel, and also runs a music label. According to online reports, Paul has a net worth of around $1 million.
