Meet Love Island's Jay Younger! His Bio, Height, Parents, Job, Age

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  857 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Love Island always has some secret contestants who later join the villa, just to shake things up. And this time around, we have Jay Younger making his appearance on the June 13, 2022 ep of the show — right after the departure of two young islanders, Liam Llewellyn and Afia Tonkmor.

So, who is he? Get to know him better as this Bio proceeds.

Jay Younger On Love Island 2022

Jay Younger was one of the two early contestants (the other being Remi Lambert)  who joined Love Island 2022 on its June 13 ep and shook a lot of bushes. But then again, this charming man was put in the latter episodes for a reason. His competitive attitude, daringness, and indecisiveness were everything that added spices to the mix.

Furthermore, he was also a “slow burner” and claimed he didn’t “fall easily”.

But this doesn’t mean, Jay was no fun. Before entering the villa he mentioned the would be “a supportive islander” who will “bring a lot of fun” and “some good laughs.”

Also, on June 13, he took it to his IG to share a teaser from his time on Love Island, captioned, “Let the Younger games begin.”

Joining him in the luxury Majorcan villa for Love Island 2022 were his co-contestants — Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu, Paige Thorne, Afia Tonkmor, Amber Beckford, Luca Bish, Andrew La Page, Gemma Owen, Davide Sanclimenti, Tasha Ghouri, Dami Hope, Indiyah Polack, Liam Llewellyn, and Ikenna Ekwonna.

Also, Missy Keating, Kat Harrison, Brad McDermott, Sophie Draper, Jordan Spencer, and Joshua Legrove were rumored to join the show in the latter half.

If case you missed the earlier episodes, this season’s contestant pairing process was a little different. As the girls arrived at the villa, they were asked to line up in front of the pool and paired with the boy that the audience chose for them. Yes! This was the first time ever the public played cupid.

Who Is Jay Younger Dating Today?

Given that Love Island is still going on (as of June 2022), Jay Younger didn’t reveal who he is dating today. Else where’s the fun in watching who he’ll end up with on the reality show.

Finding himself at a point in his life where it would be “quite fun to find someone and have fun along the way, he joined Love Island in 2022. He was looking for someone who “could add a lot of value” to his life. But didn’t have any particular preferences. However, he did mention that “someone needy” gave him quite the ick. “I quite like self-sufficiency,” Jay added.

Also, Jay was super confident about himself. He claimed to have everything the ladies in the villa wanted. “I think I am a good-looking lad and I have a lot of fun so I feel like I could add a lot of value to their lives if they add a lot of value to mine,” he explained.

Jay Younger Age

Jay Younger was 28 years of age when he appeared on Love Island in 2022.

He was the oldest contestant that season.

Jay Younger Parents

Jay Younger often featured his family in his socials. But we never saw him post about his parents. All we know is that he has a sister and his brother Tom Younger is a photographer who resided in Edinburgh, United Kingdom.

Both of his siblings were the faces behind Jay’s socials during his stint on Love Island.

Also, Jay is the grandson of the former Hibernian F.C. player Tommy Younger.

His grandfather, Tommy was an excellent goalkeeper who made his name at Hibernian, the club he joined in 1948. Their club dominated Scottish football in the early 50s and Tommy was then moved to Liverpool in 1956 for a fee of £9000.

Is Jay Younger On Instagram?

Yes, as of June 2022, find Jay on Instagram @jayyounger_ with 5.5K followers.

Also, here’s his Twitter @JayYounger_ and TikTok @jayyounger_.

Jay Younger Height

Jay Younger stands tall at a height of above 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm).

Mentioning his district features, he has brown eyes, an oblong face, and a chiseled body (that he worked hard to achieve). Infact, Jay’s a fitness enthusiast who can power lift 140 kg (308 lbs) clean and has won May 2022 Turf Games.

Furthermore, this ripped guy was also a track and field athlete during his high school days in Merchiston, Edinburgh, Scotland

Jay Younger Job

Jay Younger is an investment analyst. He took a job at Aubrey Capital Management right after graduating with first-class honors in 2017 from Herriot-Watt University. As for his major, he studied economics and finance for he was always good with numbers.

Sadly, his LinkedIn is now deleted. So, we don’t have many details about his further adventures.

For those of you wondering what his salary might be, an average investment analyst in Scotland made around $37 thousand per annum. So, this was perhaps what Jay earned as well.
