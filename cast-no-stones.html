Overall Meaning

Cody Jinks’s song Cast No Stones is a powerful message about acceptance and non-judgment. The lyrics first assert that the singer refuses to judge others, won't put up walls, but instead speaks the truth when the occasion calls. The singer admits that they don't always follow the proper way, but insists that they don't judge others who don't either. They don’t suggest that they are perfect but that they won’t cast stones despite their personal imperfections. The singer believes that God’s saving grace is available to all who seek it.


In the second verse, the singer emphasizes that their faith is personal and that while it may not be perfect, it points toward a sense of morality that shows them how to be a good person. They encourage people to abandon the idea that only one narrow interpretation of the Bible can lead to salvation, and instead the singer asks the hypothetical naysayer to think more broadly. They want to make sure that they live ethically, maintain empathy, and bring light into their world. In the end, the singer invites the listener to offer a prayer and suggests that they will see them again down the line, which is a message of hope.
