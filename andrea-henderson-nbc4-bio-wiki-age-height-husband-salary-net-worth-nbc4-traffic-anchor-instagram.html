Andrea Henderson Biography and Wiki

Andrea Henderson is an American journalist who was born in Canton, Ohio. Currently, she works as a Traffic Anchor for WCMH-TV NBC4, in Columbus, Ohio. She joined the NBC4 morning team in October 2020. Previously, before moving to Columbus,  Henderson worked as a weekend morning anchor and weekday reporter for WIINK News.

She joined the WINK news team in 2017. While at WINK News, she covered everything from Hurricane Irma and its devastating effects on the area to the major water problem that hit Southwest Florida in 2019.  Prior to joining the Fort Myers Area, she used to work as the weekend evening anchor and weekday reporter at WLIO from 2015 to 2017.
