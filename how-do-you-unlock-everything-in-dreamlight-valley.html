How do you unlock everything in Dreamlight Valley?

 Posted on September 13, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  991 words  |  Martina Birk

In order to unlock everything in Dreamlight Valley, you will need to collect a total of 41,000 Dreamlight. This can take some time, so it’s best not to try to unlock everything at once. You will also need Dreamlight to unlock doors in the castle. Below is a table with the specific amounts of Dreamlight needed to unlock each biome:

BiomeDreamlight Needed
Dazzle Beach5,000
Forest of Valor7,500
Glades of Trust6,000
Sunlit Plateau8,000
Frosted Heights9,000
Forgotten Lands5,500

Once you have collected enough Dreamlight, simply explore each biome to unlock them. Remember to also use Dreamlight to unlock doors in the castle.

Can you unlock all characters in Dreamlight Valley?

Yes, you can unlock all 29 characters in Dreamlight Valley. Each character is associated with a different realm in the Valley. Some characters can be unlocked by exploring their associated realms, while others are unlocked by progressing through the story or completing other character’s quests.

What order do you unlock biomes in Dreamlight Valley?

The recommended order to unlock the biomes in Dreamlight Valley is as follows: Dazzle Beach, Forest of Valor, Glades of Trust, Sunlit Plateau, Frosted Heights, and Forgotten Lands. However, the order can also be based on personal preference.

What to do in Dreamlight Valley with no quests?

Even without story quests, there are still plenty of tasks to be done in Dreamlight Valley. Use the opportunity to focus on other activities in the Valley, such as building friendships with characters, collecting and filling out inventories, or making new discoveries.

How much Dreamlight does it take to unlock the castle?

To unlock the Dream Castle in Dreamlight Valley, you will need to complete a series of quests. One of the quests requires you to gather 2000 Dreamlight. Once you have completed all the necessary quests, you will be able to unlock and enter the Dream Castle.

Is Belle in Dreamlight Valley?

Yes, Belle from Beauty and the Beast is available in Dreamlight Valley. She was added as part of the Enchanted Adventure update.

How much Dreamlight does it take to unlock all areas?

To unlock all the biomes in Dreamlight Valley, you will need a total of 41,000 Dreamlight. It is recommended not to try unlocking everything at once and to collect Dreamlight gradually.

Who is the forgotten character in Dreamlight Valley?

The forgotten character in Dreamlight Valley is known as The Forgotten. They were the former ruler of the Valley but fell into darkness after losing touch with their sense of wonder and magic.

Is the story finished in Dreamlight Valley?

No, the story in Dreamlight Valley is not finished. Once you have completed the main storyline, you will unlock a short series of quests with The Forgotten character. This will allow you to discover more about them and help reintegrate them into the Valley.

How do you get into the secret room in Dreamlight Valley?

To access the secret room in Dreamlight Valley, follow these steps: Enter the Castle and go right past the potted plants. Instead of going up the stairs, you will see a purple curtain. When you examine it, the curtain will pull back, revealing a door leading to the secret room.

Who lives in the pumpkin house in Dreamlight Valley?

The Fairy Godmother lives in the pumpkin house in Dreamlight Valley. She is known to be a big fan of pumpkins.

Who is in the Forgotten Lands in Dreamlight Valley?

Currently, the only character unlocked in the Forgotten Lands realm is the Fairy Godmother.

What is the max level in Dreamlight Valley?

The maximum level in Dreamlight Valley is currently level 40. There is no indication of higher levels being added to the game.

Will Daisy be in Dreamlight Valley?

Who is the villain in Dreamlight Valley?

The villains in Dreamlight Valley are Ursula the Sea Witch from The Little Mermaid and Mother Gothel from Tangled.

Is Avatar in Dreamlight Valley?

Yes, Avatar customization is available in Dreamlight Valley. You can use the Avatar Designer tool to customize your avatar’s appearance.

Why is Ursula missing in Dreamlight Valley?

If Ursula is missing in Dreamlight Valley after completing the Orb of Power quest and speaking to Merlin, try restarting the game. If she still doesn’t appear, search for her in the ponds or the sea using the in-game map.

How do you get Prince Eric in Dreamlight Valley?

To unlock Prince Eric in Dreamlight Valley, you need to attain Friendship Level 10 with Ariel and Ursula. Then, complete the Friendship Quest “A Deal with Ursula.” Afterward, you will receive the shriveled soul of Poor Unfortunate Eric. Take this item to Ariel and build Eric’s Castle by paying Scrooge McDuck 10,000 Star Coins.

Can you get more rooms in Dreamlight Valley?

Yes, you can expand and add more rooms to your house in Dreamlight Valley. Use the Scrooge sign outside your house to upgrade the house and install an “elevator.” The elevator allows you to expand room sizes and add additional floors to your house.
